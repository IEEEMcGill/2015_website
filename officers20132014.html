Mohammad Hossain Mohammadi


Roles: Chair, Webmaster

Major: Electrical Engineering, M.Eng. (Thesis)

Research Area: Electric Power Engineering

Biography: Hossain is a Research Assistant at the CompEM Lab and the Power Engineering Group at McGill University. He received his B.S.E.E with the Valedictorian award from the American University in Dubai (AUD), UAE. He was actively involved as the Chair of the AUD IEEE SB for the 2012-2013 academic year. His long-term goal at the McGill IEEE SB is to involve new engineering students with the IEEE spirit.

Ahmad Muannaki


Role: Vice-Chair Academic

Major: Electrical Engineering, B.Eng.

Minor: Software Engineering

Biography: Ahmad is a 3rd year Electrical Engineering student who is currently working as a Software Engineering Intern at Mannarino Systems and Software Inc. His research interests are Signal Processing and Information Theory. His hobbies include cycling and playing soccer.

Reza Abdolee


Role: Vice-Chair External

Major: Electrical Engineering, PhD

Research Area: Telecommunication and Signal Processing

Biography: Reza Abdolee is currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, McGill University. In 2012, he was a research scholar at the Bell Labs, Alcatel-Lucent, Stuttgart, Germany. In 2011, he was a visiting Ph.D. student at the Department of Electrical Engineering, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). His research interests include communication theory, statistical signal processing, optimization, and hardware design and integration.

Malika Meghjani


Role: Students' Activities Representative

Major: Computer Science, PhD

Biography: Malika is a PhD student at the School of Computer Science. Her research interests are in Robotics and Computer Vision. She is the Students' Activities Representative for both McGill IEEE Student Branch and IEEE Montreal Section. She is also the Chapter Chair of Robotics and Automation Society at the Montreal Section. Recently, she is working with Women in Engineering and Computer Science Society at McGill.

Kai Xiong


Role: Treasurer

Major: Computer Science, M.Sc. (Thesis)

Research Area: Computer Networking

Biography: Kai Xiong is a M.Sc. candidate at the Cyber Physical System Lab, School of Computer Science at McGill University. He received his B.Sc. from Politecnico di Torino, Italy in 2012. His research interests include Dedicated Short-Range Communication, Avionics Full-Duplex Switched Ethernet and Software Defined Networks. Before joining McGill, he had been with Alcatel-Lucent Bell Labs (France), Wireless Sensor Network Lab at Polito (Italy) and Ultra-fast Nonlinear Optics group at DTU (Denmark).

Ming Ma


Role: Secretary

Major: Electrical Engineering, PhD

Research Area: All-Optical Signal Processing based on Silicon Photonics

Biography: Ming is a PhD candidate at the Photonics System Group. He finished his Bachelors program in China and Masters program in Denmark. He has 4 years working experience in the industry of IT and mobile phone.

Shohreh Shaghaghian


Role: Women in Engineering (WIE) Representative

Major: Electrical Engineering, PhD

Research Area: Computer Networking

Biography: Shohreh is a PhD candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, McGill University, Montreal, QC, Canada. She received her B.Sc. and M.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2010 and 2012, respectively. Her research interests are in the area of computer and telecommunication network analysis.

Zahra Karimaghaloo


Role: Women in Engineering (WIE) Representative

Major: Electrical Engineering, PhD

Research Area: Medical Image Processing

Biography: Zahra's PhD research at the Medical Image Analysis Lab and the Center for Intelligent Machines at McGill University, under supervision of Dr. Tal Arbel, involves investigating new techniques for segmentation of active lesions in brain MRIs of patients with Multiple sclerosis. Her Masters work at the University of Queen's introduced new methods for seed localization problem in prostate brachytherapy. She holds a B.A in Electrical Engineering from Tehran University.

Dena Firoozi


Role: Women in Engineering (WIE) Representative

Major: Electrical Engineering, PhD

Research Area: Control Systems

Biography: Dena is PhD student at the Centre for Intelligent Machines (CIM) at McGill University. Her research interests include estimation theory, stochastic control and robotics. She completed her M.Sc. at the Sharif University of Technology, Iran, and her B.Sc. at Shiraz University, Iran.

Ali Jahanbani Ardakani


Role: 2013-2015 IEEE Canada Student Representative

Major: Electrical Engineering, PhD

Research Area: Electric Power Systems

Biography: Ali is a PhD candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at McGill University. He received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. from Tehran Polytechnic in 2005 and 2008, respectively. His research interests include renewable energies, smart grids and power system operation and planning.

Prof. Roni Khazaka


Associate Professor, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering

Roles: Student Branch Counselor, IEEE Montreal Section Chair

External Website
