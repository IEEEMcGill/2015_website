Celebrate Women in Technology


The McGill IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) and Computer Science Affinity Group, would like to invite you all for celebrating the contributions of women in technology.



Join us for this fun and informative event to:

  • Meet our very own: Principal Dr. Suzanne Fortier
  • Commemorate technology pioneers: Grace Hopper & Anita Borg
  • Learn how to create and 3D print your designs
  • Participate in a 3D printing design contest and win a chance to print your own design!
The details of the event are given below
  • Date: 25th March 2015, Wednesday
  • Time: 2 pm - 5 pm
  • Location: FDA 5
