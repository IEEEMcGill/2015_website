It was a great success for McGill and Montreal as 3 teams out of top 6 of Canada and the top 3 teams of Montreal were from McGill University! Best Student Branch in IEEE Montreal Section

During the 2014 IEEE Montreal Awards Banquet & AGM, the McGill IEEE Student Branch was awarded the "Best Student Branch" within the IEEE Montreal Section. IEEE R7 Director & Canada President and the IEEE Montreal Section presented this award to Mohammad Hossain Mohammadi, 2014-2015 Chair, who also collected the 2014 IEEE Montreal Section's Best Student Branch, 2014 IEEE R7 Student Branch Website Design Contest, and 2013 IEEE Student Branch Web Site Contest. Congratulations to the 2013-2014 branch officers!



2014 IEEE R7 Student Branch Website Design Contest

IEEE R7 Director & Canada President and IEEE Canada Student Activities Chair awarded the 1st place prize to the McGill IEEE Student Branch website in the 2014 IEEE R7 Student Branch Website Design Contest. Congratulations to all branch officers and those involved in helping improve the website's content! A special thanks to Mohammad Hossain Mohammadi, our 2013-14 Webmaster, for frequently updating our website and receiving the award certificate during the IEEE R7 Student Congress on September 20th 2014 at the Toronto Airport West hotel, Mississauga, ON.


Active 3D Printing at our McNaughton Center

Since the re-opening of our McNaughton Center in January 2014, we have been actively involved in printing more than 40 different 3D models for McGill students and faculty, both IEEE and non-IEEE members. A great thanks to the IEEE Canadian Foundation (ICF) for providing us with the 2013 McNaughton Center grant. We offer 3D Printing & Equipment Rental services to interested students and faculty within the IEEE Montreal Section.



Successful 2013 Year rounded by 3 Branch Awards

During the IEEE Montreal AGM & Banquet 2013, three prestigious student awards were given to our McGill IEEE Student Branch and WIE affinity group. Mojtaba Vaezi, 2012-2013 Chair, collected both the IEEE Montreal Section's Best Student Branch Award 2013 and the 2013 Region 7 Larry K. Wilson Student Activities Award. We were also proud to have Malika Meghjani collect the 2013 IEEE Canada WIE Prize. Congratulations to the 2012-2013 officers!


2013 Global IEEE Student Branch Website Contest

MGA student activities committee has announced that the McGill IEEE Student Branch website has achieved the 3rd place in the Global IEEE Student Branch website contest in 2013. Congratulations to everybody and thanks to those who helped improve the content of this website. A special thanks to Sina Hamidi, our 2012-13 webmaster, for his excellent job. The Web is an exciting and engaging means of staying in contact with IEEE student members all around the globe and the recent proliferation of McGill IEEE Student Branch is highly related not just to Sina's outstanding website design, but also his timely actions.

2013 Region 7 Larry K. Wilson Student Activities Award

Congratulations to Mojtaba Vaezi, McGill IEEE branch chair in 2012-2013, for winning the Region 7 (Canada) Larry K. Wilson Student Activities Award in 2013. This award is to recognize the student member most responsible for an extraordinary accomplishment, in each Region of the IEEE.

As a key member of McGill IEEE student branch from 2011 to 2013, Mojtaba has shown a pattern of dedication and ongoing service to McGill Student branch and IEEE Montreal. The image of the branch has completely changed during the past year and the branch experienced an exceptional growth in its student members, events and activities, and funding. Specifically, by securing eight national grants/awards, either institutional or personal, McGill IEEE student branch has flourished under his leadership during 2012-2013.

2013 IEEE Canada Women in Engineering Prize

IEEE Canada annually awards the IEEE Canada Women in Engineering prize to recognize the excellence in young women engineer professionals. It is awarded to a person who has demonstrated substantial academic success and volunteer service to the IEEE.

This year, the prize was awarded to Malika Meghjani, Students' Activities and Women in Engineering representative for McGill IEEE Student Branch, 2012-2013.

Malika played a key role in re-establishing the McGill IEEE Student branch with two other former members in 2010. In past she was the Vice-Chair for the branch, 2011-2012. She has also been active in IEEE Montreal Section. Her roles with the IEEE Montreal Section as Students' Activities Representative and Chapter Chair (2011-2013) provided her the opportunity to organize Montreal Section wide events from field trips and programming competitions to women in engineering workshop and technical talks. These events have helped her develop leadership, organization, communication and coordination skills that will be valuable to her academic research.
