Mohammad Hossain Mohammadi


Roles: Chair

Major: Electrical Engineering, M.Eng. (Thesis)

Research Area: Electric Power, Computational Electromagnetics

Biography: Hossain's research focus is on the "Comparative Design & Optimization of Novel Synchronous Reluctance Motors for Traction Applications" at the Computational Electromagnetics Lab. He received his B.S.E.E with the Valedictorian award from the American University in Dubai (AUD), UAE. He was actively involved as Chair of the AUD IEEE SB for 2012-2013 and Webmaster of the McGill IEEE SB for 2013-2014. His long-term goal at the McGill IEEE SB is to help create a "fun learning environment" for interested students in STEM fields.
